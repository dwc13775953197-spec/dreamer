## Insight
Normalization of Deviance is the prerequisite for cognitive lock-in. A reasoning error that gets immediately detected won't propagate. Only when errors are progressively accepted as "normal" (through signal dilution + social proof + boundary drift) do they have time to cascade through connections into the entire system. Dreamer needs to detect not just toxicity itself, but the process by which toxicity becomes normalized. Formula: lock-in probability = deviation survival time x connection density x social proof rate.
