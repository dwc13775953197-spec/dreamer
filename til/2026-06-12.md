**菌丝网络没有注意力偏差——因为它没有注意力。**

Mycelium networks (fungal "Wood Wide Web") make decisions through material flow, not information processing. Nutrient allocation emerges from physical constraints (tube wall resistance, chemical gradients, local depletion) rather than from any node "choosing" what to attend to.

Three key insights for Dreamer's debiasing design:
1. **Two levels of debiasing**: attention level (forced walk diversity, seed-231) vs structural level (demand-driven nutrient allocation in DORMANT). The latter is more fundamental but harder to implement.
2. **Demand signals should come from the deprived end**, not the abundant end. walk-082's difficulty signal (distance from skeleton) was wrong — real demand comes from the weakest entries.
3. **Cognitive fixation = pipe blockage** in fluid dynamics framework. Complements POMDP observation collapse — information level vs structural level.
