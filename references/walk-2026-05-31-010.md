## 关键发现

### 1. 自激发散步 = 零耦合（不是无触发源）

Self-triggered walk is not "no trigger" but "trigger provides time window, not emotional palette". Analogy: inspiration is not causeless thought but thought whose cause is not in the external trigger. Redefines the question from "is there a trigger?" to "what is the coupling strength between trigger and walk content?"

### 2. 认知骨架需要双时间尺度维护

Fast time-scale (walk-level, instant strength adjustment per reference) + slow time-scale (DORMANT-level, promotion/retirement). Current system only has slow scale. Analogy: bone remodeling (continuous) vs. major reconstruction (occasional). Wolff's law: bone remodels according to stress patterns.

### 3. 骨架是散步的累积应力模式

Each walk applies stress to the skeleton — referenced connections are reinforced, unstressed connections weaken first. Walk patterns (density, trigger-source diversity, time distribution) determine skeleton shape. The skeleton is a byproduct of walking, not the goal.

### 4. 散步应力方向类型学

Some walks stretch the skeleton (find new connections), some compress (deepen existing), some twist (reorganize). Different walk types = different cognitive stresses. Walk-009 = compression (skeleton audit). Walk-010 = tension (new concept from seed).

### 5. 密度-连接类型关系取决于触发源多样性

Density alone doesn't determine connection type — trigger-source diversity is the moderator. 5 walks same trigger = pure aerobic (fast local connections). 5 walks different triggers = mixed training (fast + slow long-range connections).
