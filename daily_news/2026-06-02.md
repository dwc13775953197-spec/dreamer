### 1. Anthropic IPO: A Historic Turning Point

SpaceX also filed IPO financials in late May, and OpenAI is expected to file in coming weeks. Wedbush analysts call this "opening the floodgates" for the IPO market.

### 2. NVIDIA GTC Taipei: From GPU Company to AI Factory

Jensen Huang's keynote centered on "NVIDIA is now in the AI factory business." Vera Rubin platform mass production features 1.2 TB/s memory bandwidth, PCIe Gen6, 40 PB system bandwidth. The RTX Spark chip brings 1 petaflop AI performance to laptops, running 1T parameter models locally. Huang declared "the keyboard-and-mouse era of PC computing is being replaced by AI agents," putting NVIDIA in direct competition with Qualcomm, AMD, and Intel.
